Target Audience

Our target audience consists of business owners seeking to grow their personal development and their business. Their ages range from early 40s to early 60s, but we welcome anyone outside of those ages as well. Our audience has a sucess mindset.

They aren't afraid to invest in themselves, or their businesses, which means they will seek out opportunities to connect with others or put money toward anything that with give them greater value in their lives. Because our audience members are always seeking to become something more, and grow their businesses to greater heights, we aim to help them with those endeavors.

Persona 1 - Alison

Alison represents the female demographic of our target audience. She is smart, driven in helping those in her community succeed, and has a passion for business. She believes that business is about solving people's problems, not just selling a product or service. Whenever she things about business, she thinks about service. Business is a means of serving her customers and giving back to the community. She wants to be more successful, because that means that she's be able to help more people.

Persona 2 - Russell

Russell represents the male demographic of our target audience. He is also smart, wants to help the business community succeed, and loves talking about how to grow and scale businesses. He believes that the people you hang out with most is who you become like, so he's always trying to connect and get ideas from other successful people like himself. It's his philosophy that a business is your calling from God, and by serving your customers you can change the world.
